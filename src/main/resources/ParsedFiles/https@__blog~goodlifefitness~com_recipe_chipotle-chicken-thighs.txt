Add the chicken and mix until the thighs are uniformly coated. Let marinade for a minimum of 1 hour, optimally overnight. Grill the thighs on a preheated barbecue over medium-high heat for 12-18 minutes (depending on size), or until an internal temperature of 165° has been reached. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
